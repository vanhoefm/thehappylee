About Happy

Hi! I'm Happy, the illustrator/designer and artist behind this happylee. I am a self-taught and an independent artist.

I have made this website, where you can see my creations and sneak a peek into my latest work and hire me for your next project if our ideas collide.

Why I'm called Happy, I was born on New Years Eve! And I guess I gathered all the happy vibes that day. That's where happylee came from, a combination of my real name (Darlee) and nickname (Happy). And yes, I smile a lot!

My goal is simply to keep myself busy using creativity and make others happy through my work.

Thank you so much for following along, don't hesitate to write me if you have any questions or suggestions at happypingay@gmail.com.

Can't wait to work with you!
